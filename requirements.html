What requirements needed to rent a car?

Walk- in Application

  • 1. Be at least 23 years old: Most car rental companies in the Philippines have an age requirement of at least 23 years old for renters. Some companies may allow drivers under 23 to rent a car, but they may charge an additional fee or require additional insurance.
  • 2. Have a valid driver’s license: To rent a car in the Philippines, you will need to present a valid driver’s license. This should be a license that is issued by your home country or an international driver’s license.
  • 3. Have a credit card: Most car rental companies in the Philippines require a credit card to hold the rental and for any additional charges that may arise during your rental period.
  • 4. Meet any additional requirements: Some car rental companies in the Philippines may have additional requirements, such as a minimum rental period or a deposit. Be sure to check with the rental company to ensure that you meet all of their requirements before renting a car.
